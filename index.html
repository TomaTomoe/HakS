About project

Hacken is the first cybersecurity expertise decentralized cryptocurrency, build on Ethereum.

Hacken is the first cyber security expertise decentralized cryptocurrency, build on Ethereum. It creates the ecosystem for providing high quality cyber security services for the companies all around the world. The Token sale is powered by the CyberGuard Company. CyberGuard accumulates and distributes the investments, controls the processes and develops the HackenProof platform a marketplace for the cyber security services.

The platform will allow users to purchase penetration test services for their products or systems. HackenProof will provide clients with the o fficial digital mark and certificate in case of successful completion of cybersecurity test.

The tests will be run by the best teams of us white hackers.
